After seeing many ads for [Mintlify](https://www.mintlify.com/), "The knowledge infrastructure agents build on", I decided to give it a try.

I just needed to head to [mintlify.wiki](https://mintlify.wiki) and enter my GitHub repository link: `github.com/tinkerer9/CollaboKeys`. Mintlify took a half-hour to generate my documentation.

After a bit of quick research, it seems like Mintlify is completely free for these purposes/
Its [Starter Plan](https://www.mintlify.com/pricing) is 100% free and includes these functions:

- *Full platform*
- *Custom domain*
- *Web editor*
- *Authentication*
- *Assistant*
- *Writing agent*
- *Automations*
- *MCP server*

Once the documentation finished generating, I was redirected to my personal Mintlify URL.
**Check it out here: [mintlify.wiki/tinkerer9/CollaboKeys](https://mintlify.wiki/tinkerer9/CollaboKeys)**

Mintlify automatically updates the wiki to match CollaboKeys changes.
I found Mintlify to be an easy and powerful documentation generator.
